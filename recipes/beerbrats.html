Beer Bratwurst

These awesome brats are simmered in beer with onions and spices to make them bursting with flavor before finishing them off on the grill. Place the grilled brats in hot dog rolls, and top them with the tender onions and mustard, or serve them alongside German potato salad.

Ingredients

Make sure you have these on hand before starting

  • 4 (12 ounce) cans beer
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 10 bratwurst
  • 2 teaspoons red pepper flakes
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

How to Make Beer Brats

Ready for a mouthwatering, handheld meal that comes together in under 30 minutes? You'll find the full recipe below with step-by-step instructions, but here's what you can expect when you make this top-rated recipe:

  1. Combine beer and onion slices in a large pot; bring to a boil
  2. Add bratwurst, red peppe flakes, garlic powder, salt and ground black pepper to beer and onion mixture. Reduce heat to medium and simmer for 10 to 12 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  4. Transfew bratwurst to a plate. Reduce heat to low and let onions simmer until needed.
  5. Cook bratwurst on the preheated grill, turning occasionally to get char marks, until no longer pink in the middle, 5 to 10 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
  6. Serve bratwurst with onions.
